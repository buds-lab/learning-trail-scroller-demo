NZEB@SDE
NZEB@SDE is the first purpose-built Net Zero Energy Building(NZEB) commissioned by a Singapore tertiary institution. The following text describes its various design features.
Separate Building Masses
Splitting SDE4's massing into smaller volumes gives its occupants greater access to light and natural ventilation.
Informal Learning
SDE4 encourages learning through interaction and collaboration between occupants by providing ample connecting and break out spaces.
