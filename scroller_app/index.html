SDE Learning Trail @ SDE4
SDE Learning Trail is a simple, easy to use web application to collect comfort feedback from building occupants and visitors while they interact, learn and engage with SDE4.
NZEB @ SDE
NZEB@SDE is Singapore's first purpose-built Net Zero Energy Building(NZEB). SDE4 was launched officially by Minister Heng Swee Keat on 30th of January, 2019.
