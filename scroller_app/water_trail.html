Water
Water is a precondition for living systems and an instrument of mental and spiritual well-being. Effective management of water - quality and volume - is critical towards achieving net zero energy targets for buildings in the tropics. SDE4 uses various strategies for water management while integrating water sensitive design features with land development.
ABC Water Programme
Active, Beautiful, Clean(ABC) Waters programme outlines natural purification processes to protect water quality, enhance biodiversity and reduce flow of stormwater runoff from the built landscape. SDE4 is a ABC Waters site which uses a combination of on-site retention and rainwater harvesting strategies to bring the community closer to water.

Bio Retention Basin
At SDE4, one third of the roof rainwater runoff is conveyed into the bioretention basin which slows down the stormwater run-off, embellishes the landscape and filters water through a dense vegetation layer. This harvested water is then used for irrigation and other non potable water demands of the building.
