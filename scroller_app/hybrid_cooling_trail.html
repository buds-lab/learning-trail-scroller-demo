Hybrid Cooling
SDE4 uses a mixed-mode hybrid cooling system, that maintains indoor air quality and optimum cooling of spaces while minimising energy consumption.
Adaptive Comfort
SDE4 offers direct control over immediate environment through openable windows and adjustable fans speeds - which helps users adapt to a wider range of conditions, beyond conventional thermal comfort boundaries in the tropics.
Mixed Mode
SDE4's hybrid cooling system is a single-pass system that supplies rooms with pre-cooled air - at higher temperatures and humidity levels than a conventional system - and augments it with elevated air speed by ceiling fans.
