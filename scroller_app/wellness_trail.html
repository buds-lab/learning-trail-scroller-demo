Well Certification
SDE4 has achieved a gold certification mainly for its achievements in air and lighting. SDE4 has maximised these through the floating boxes concept, where a shallow plan depth and porous layout allows for cross-breezes and natural lighting.
Lighting
With diffused natural light entering the building, SDE4 allows the occupants internal clock to remain synchronized, improving sleep patterns and boosting occupant health and wellbeing.
Air
SDE4 incorporates fresh air through natural ventilation of classroom spaces. However, if air quality outside is not favourable, operable windows allow the class to utilise the hybrid cooling system instead.
Movement
Put simply, physical activity matters for more than just our physical health. The intent of the Movement concept in WELL is to promote movement, physical activity and active living and discourage sedentary behaviors. To facilitate movement, SDE4 integrates strategies such as active staircases, with no escalators included on site.
